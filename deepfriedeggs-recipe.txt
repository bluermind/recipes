Deep Fried Eggs - Recipe

Ingriedients
- 2 Eggs
- Canola Oil or any oil you think will do for cooking
- Salt

1. Pour oil to cover all areas of the pan with about half a centimeter thickness
2. Turn on the stove and heat the pan. 
3. Break the eggs into the oil
4. Make sure you are wearing long sleeve clothes and gloves to safeguard yourself from the oil bursts
5. Wait till eggs become deep fried like French Fries
6. Use a metallic gauze or similar to rescue your eggs from the pan
7. Add a little salt to your fried eggs.
8. You might wanna clean your kitchen (floors and stoves and nearby items)
